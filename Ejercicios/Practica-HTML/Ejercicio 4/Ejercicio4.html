Receta de Cocina

  1. Ingredientes:
    • 375g spaghetti
    • 18.20 gm olive oil
    • 1 brown onion, finely chopped
    • 1 carrot, peeled, finely chopped
    • 500g 3 Star Beef Mince
    • 1 small red capsicum, seeded, finely chopped
    • 2 garlic cloves, crushed
    • 22.20 gm Italian Tomato Paste
    • 400g can Diced Tomatoes
    • 185ml beef stock
    • 20g parmesan cheese
  2. Cook spaghetti: Bring a large saucepan of boiling water to a boil. Cook spaghetti according to packet directions until al dente. Drain well.
  3. Brown the mince: Heat oil in a large frying pan over high heat. Add onion and cook for 3-4 minutes or until softened. Add carrot and cook for a further 2 minutes. Add mince and cook, stirring with a wooden spoon to break up any lumps, for 5 minutes or until browned. Add capsicum and garlic and cook for 2 minutes.
  4. Add tomato paste and stock: Add tomato paste, diced tomatoes, and beef stock. Bring to the boil, then reduce heat to low and simmer, uncovered, for 10-12 minutes or until sauce thickens.
  5. Season and serve: Season with salt and pepper to taste. Serve spaghetti topped with bolognese sauce and parmesan cheese.
